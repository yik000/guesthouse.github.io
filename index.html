About

Welcome to the Carrauntoohil guesthouse, a small five star mountain lodge nestled in the Macgillycuddy's Reeks. A perfect location for someone looking for a quiet retreat or for an avid hill-walker.

Rooms

At Carrauntoohil guesthouse we offer a choice of three fully en-suite rooms. Each lavish room comes with its own unique outstanding view of the surrounding area. Breakfast is also included in the price of the room.

What to do

The main attraction here is hiking. Being only a two hour hike from Carrauntoohil peak the Guesthouse offers itself as a perfect rest stop. Stunning helicopter rides are also available and the small town of Killarney is only an hour's drive away.
