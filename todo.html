The Area

The Macgillycuddy's Reeks mountain range is the highest in Ireland. Within it are nine summits which are over 900m above sea-level. The peak of Carrauntoohil is the highest point in Ireland at 1,041m.

The buzzing town of Killarney is almost an hour's drive away and offers up many tourist attractions such as Muckross House & Gardens and Torc Waterfall. If you choose to have a long stay with us we urge you to take a day to visit this virbrant town.


Things to Do

The main activity here is hiking. The Carrauntoohil summit is only a two hour hike from the Guesthouse.
Also at Carrauntoohil Guesthouse we offer a fabulous helicopter ride around the mountain range. This awe-inspiring journey allows you to easily take in the astonishing picturesque landscape. We highly recommend hiking to the summit before embarking on this ride of a lifetime.
