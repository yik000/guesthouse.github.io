Who are We

We are a small, professionally run family guesthouse located over 3,000 feet above sea level on Carrauntoohil mountain in Co. Kerry, Ireland. We were established in 1967 and have been accommodating visitors ever since.


Food

Breakfast is included in the price of every room. At Carrauntoohil Guesthouse our in-house Michelin star chef prepares a diverse and divine breakfast each morning. Choose anything from a full Irish Breakfast to a Continental. All the food served at the Guesthouse is organic and locally sourced.

Location

Carrauntoohil Guesthouse is located along the Macgillycuddy's Reeks mountain range in County Kerry in Ireland. The Guesthouse can be easily accessed by helicopter, by foot or by any off-road vehicle. The Guesthouse has various dirt trails leading to it and is extremely well sign-posted.
