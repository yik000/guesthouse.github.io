At Carrauntoohil Guesthouse we offer a selection of three ensuite rooms:

  1. Master Double Room

  2. Twin Single Room

  3. Double and Single Room

Every elegant bathroom has a shower, bath, sink and toilet.

Each of the three rooms come with:
  1. Complimentary Breakfast

  2. Breathtaking views

  3. A Widescreen TV

  4. Free wifi

  5. Tea & Coffee making facilites


Master Double Room

Price per one night: €87

This is our most deluxe room, best suited for a couple. The south facing window gives a stunning view over the Bridia Valley. The king size double bed is garnished with four pillows and a duvet containing the finest ostrich feathers offering endless sleep filled nights.



Twin Single Room

Price per one night: €63

An elegant room containing two hand-made artisan single beds. This room has both north and west facing windows offering astonishing views of not just the lowlands of Glencar but also, to the north, the peaceful Lough Eagher.



Double and Single Room

Price per one night: €71

This is our only room with a view towards Carrauntoohil peak. This room is perfect for a family of three or a couple with a mutual best friend. The expertly crafted mahogany desk offers a perfect location to document your wonderous stay.
